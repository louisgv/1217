Rule

Choosing an avatar

Player initially choose an avatar in a set of 5 unique avatars. Each avatar represents an alchemy element (Ether, Water, Fire, Aero, Earth). By choosing an avatar, the player is applying a higher probability of drawing a card of the same alchemy element to the avatar.

The card player chose will also determine whether if they would be the first to make a move or not. In short, it is a 5-ways rock-paper-scisor with the NPC's randomly chosen avatar. The player won if the element chosen beats the NPC's element according to the chart below, or if it is a draw.

  • ETHER beats WATER and FIRE
  • WATER beats FIRE and AERO
  • FIRE beats AERO and EARTH
  • AERO beats EARTH and ETHER
  • EARTH beats ETHER and WATER
