A digital pet game where the pet is a swarm of growing maggot and player feed them by droping unique images file into the game.

gameplay

Mechanics

Play will be in charge of 9 initial maggots. They must feed these maggot with unique images then can find on the internet, or their personal images. The catch is, they cannot feed the same image twice! The area (in pixel) of the food item will be for internal calculation. The maximum width of a food item will be cramped down to 100px. The height will be scaled by the original ratio accordingly.

Control

Mouse to drag and drop image from the desktop to the game view. The file droped must be an image of either PNG, JPEG, maybe small GIF.

Onboarding

When player first arrived, they will see 9 maggots crawling on their screen, with instruction text on the left side telling them how to feed the maggots.
